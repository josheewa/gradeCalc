Grade Calculator

  1. Make sure you are on a computer, not a cellphone
  2. Open Synergy
  3. Navigate to Grade Book
  4. Click the class you wish to calculate
  5. Select all the text on the screen [Ctrl+A (Windows), Cmd+A (macOS)]
  6. Copy that to clipboard [Ctrl+C (Windows), Cmd+C (macOS)]
  7. Come back here, and paste the raw text into the text box, and press Enter or click Submit
